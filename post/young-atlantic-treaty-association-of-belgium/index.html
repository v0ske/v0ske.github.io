Young Atlantic Treaty Association of Belgium

YATA

I am also actively engaged in YATA Belgium, the Young Atlantic Treaty Association, which serves as the youth partner organization of NATO and was established five years after the alliance itself. The Belgian chapter has been active since 1961 and was founded by former Prime Minister Paul van Zeeland.

The mission of YATA is to promote transatlantic cooperation, strengthen democratic values, the rule of law, and security, and bridge the gap between policy and civil society through dynamic youth engagement in international security and defense.

Following the YATA Belgium board elections on December 1, 2024, I was elected as President. Initially, I will serve as “President Elect” until the board of ATA Belgium (Euro-Atlantic Association Belgium) formally confirms our new YATA Belgium board. The date of this meeting is yet to be determined.
